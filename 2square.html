2SQUARE DESIGN
2SQUARE DESIGN is a project of an effective CMS interface design for a high-end design store. The project seeks to streamline business processes, incorporate responsive design and elevate the user experience.
Goal
The CMS design looks to incorporate design principles, accessibility guidelines and human-centred design thinking to produce a considerate, responsive and effective content management system interface and experience. This project places a focus on UX Research integration, pinpointing client frustration areas in their current business methods and respecting their workflow.
