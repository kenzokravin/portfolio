HIKER
HIKER is a hiking application aimed to foster community and hiking progression. Whether it's seeing what your friends are up to, recording your group hike memories or just needing to know where to go, HIKER is there to provide assistance for hikers of all levels.
Goal
HIKER aims to support all forms of hiking. Combining a social network, park services and trail maps into one sleek, easy-to-use application, HIKER looks to remove the fuss of hiking struggles. Primarily a UI project, this project addresses accessibility and responsiveness through incorporating key design principles, ensuring the user experience is readable, usable and effective.
