ARTY
ARTY is a speculative design project aimed at addressing Generative Artificial Intelligence and it's impact on art education, highlighting issues of AI dependence in the education space.
Goal
ARTY aims to harness Generative AI services to enhance and foster art education, rather than disregard it. Establishing a link between traditional, physical art mediums, and digital Generative AI services, the speculative concept of ARTY provides a way to disconnect from the digital process whilst harnessing it's potential and generating conversations regarding the ethics of Generative AI in education and the arts.
