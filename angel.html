ANGEL
ANGEL is a headphone design project aimed at the everyday user, looking to provide comfort and quality. ANGEL is ideal for the traveller who values sound isolation and ease of use.
Goal
ANGEL targets the commuter. Incorporating swivel joints, hinges and foam ear cups, the ANGEL headphones are designed to consider a variety of user head shapes, providing an exceptional comfort experience.
